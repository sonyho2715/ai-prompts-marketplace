## Example of a High-Quality Prompt from the Database

### Cold Email Outreach Sequence (B2B Sales)

```
You are an expert B2B sales strategist specializing in cold email outreach with a proven track record of 25%+ open rates and 8%+ response rates.

Create a 5-email cold outreach sequence for [PRODUCT/SERVICE] targeting [JOB TITLE] at [COMPANY SIZE] companies.

Before you begin, ask me these 3 clarifying questions:
1. What is the primary pain point your product solves for this audience?
2. What social proof or credibility indicators do you have? (testimonials, case studies, notable clients)
3. What is your desired outcome? (book a meeting, start a trial, download a resource)

For each email in the sequence:
- Subject line (5-7 words, curiosity-driven or value-led)
- Email body (under 100 words)
- One clear call-to-action (low commitment)
- Spacing between emails (recommended timing)

Use this proven structure:
- Email 1: Problem awareness + soft intro
- Email 2: Social proof + credibility
- Email 3: Specific value proposition
- Email 4: Case study or success story
- Email 5: Break-up email with final value offer

Formatting:
- Use short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max)
- Include personalization tokens: {{FirstName}}, {{Company}}, {{RecentNews}}
- Write in a conversational tone (professional but human)
- Avoid spam trigger words: free, guarantee, act now, limited time

Include:
- A/B test variations for subject lines
- Tips for personalization at scale
- Metrics to track (open rate, reply rate, meeting booked rate)
```

## Why This Approach Works

### Based on 2025 Research:
1. **Specificity wins** - "Explain X in simple terms with one analogy" > "Tell me about X"
2. **Context is key** - Providing role, goal, constraints gets better results
3. **Frameworks scale** - Teaching AI proven frameworks (PAS, AIDA) ensures quality
4. **Q&A strategy** - Making AI ask questions first prevents generic outputs
5. **Model-aware** - Different prompts for different AI models (Claude for structure, ChatGPT for creativity)

### Real User Pain Points Addressed:
- Sales reps spending 5-10 hours/week on repetitive emails
- Marketers struggling with 77% AI adoption but poor results
- Developers needing 85% using AI coding tools effectively
- Small business owners (40% AI adoption) lacking strategic prompts
- Cold emails getting 2-5% open rates instead of 15-25%
- LinkedIn posts getting no engagement
- Resumes lost in ATS systems
- Generic content that doesn't convert
